Drag and Drop Support

Keptab has fully supported drag and drop. You can drag and drop tabs in your Keptab list to reorder them or merge them.

Re-order Tabs

Re-order tabs means that you can drag tab item from re-ordering, for example, drag fisrt item to the second, and the smae, it also means that you can drag tab item from A tabs group to B tabs group.

Merge Tabs

Merging tabs supported in Keptab. You can drag the total count text that says e.g. "5 tabs" to another tabs total count text . This will merge two tabs group into one tabs group.
